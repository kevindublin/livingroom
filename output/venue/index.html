The Space

Manny's is a community focused meeting and learning place in San Francisco's Mission District. It houses a non-profit restaurant that partners with Farming Hope, a political bookshop, and consistently hosts civic events & music. It's open Tuesday - Friday: 11AM - 7PM and Saturday - Sunday: 10AM-7PM. You should join their mailing list to keep up to date with all that's going on.
